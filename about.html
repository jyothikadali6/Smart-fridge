🌟 Our Mission

At Smart Fridge, our mission is to create an efficient way to keep track of groceries and reduce food waste.

💡 What We Offer

  • ✅ Item Tracking – Add and manage food items with quantity and expiry date.
  • ✅ Expiry Notifications – Get email alerts before food expires.
  • ✅ User-Friendly Experience – Simple and interactive UI for easy usage.
  • ✅ Secure Access – Login with your email and manage your fridge privately.
